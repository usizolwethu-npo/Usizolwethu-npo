Welcome

Usizolwethu Organization is a Non-Profit Organization (NPO) dedicated to supporting vulnerable people in our community through feeding programmes, youth development, and community support initiatives.

Our goal is to bring hope, dignity, and opportunity to those in need by working together with volunteers, donors, and partners.

About Us

Usizolwethu Organization NPO was established to respond to the growing needs within our community. We believe that everyone deserves access to basic needs, care, and opportunities for a better future.

Our Mission

To uplift and empower communities through sustainable support programmes that promote dignity, hope, and development.

Our Vision

A caring and self-reliant community where no one goes hungry and everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Values

  • Compassion
  • Transparency
  • Community Empowerment
  • Integrity

What We Do

Feeding Programme

We provide meals to vulnerable individuals and families, especially during community events and outreach programmes.

Youth Development

We support young people through guidance, skills development activities, and positive community engagement.

Community Support

We assist community members during times of need through donations, awareness campaigns, and partnerships.
